In 2024, despite my thriving career as a machine learning engineer, researcher, and deep learning developer, I felt a deep sense of stagnation. I was juggling projects and ideas, yet something was missing—true balance and fulfillment in my life.

I decided it was time to take a closer look at what truly mattered. I immersed myself in countless books, articles, and documentaries. Every resource taught me something new—even if much of it didn’t stick, there were golden nuggets of wisdom that reshaped my outlook on life and work.

Realizing that the insights I was gathering could help others who felt stuck too, I launched my blog. This space became my personal repository of learnings—a living document of my evolution as a constant learner. It’s a chronicle of every step I took toward finding balance and fulfillment.

My story is far from over. Every day, I continue to push the boundaries in machine learning and beyond, always on the lookout for new ideas and fresh perspectives. This relentless pursuit of learning not only defines my career but also fuels my passion for life—a passion I’m eager to share with everyone who follows my journey.
