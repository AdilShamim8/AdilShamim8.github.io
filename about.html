Adil’s Journey: From Dreams to Reality

Adil, a 20-year-old student from Bangladesh, dreamed big—bigger than most. With aspirations of studying at Harvard University and becoming an ML engineer, his life was a canvas waiting for bold strokes of success.

The Beginning

Adil woke up every day at 6 AM, filled with a sense of purpose. His mornings were not about rushing to keep up with the world but about setting the pace for his ambitions. A 15-hour winter routine became his mantra, where five hours were dedicated solely to Machine Learning (ML), 1.5 hours to academic studies, and another 1.5 hours to book reading. English learning and LeetCode challenges also found their rightful place in his daily schedule.

Adil’s passion for coding had no bounds. He started his journey by creating simple yet elegant websites. His GitHub became a treasure trove of projects like his Tic-Tac-Toe game and the ‘50 Days of Machine Learning’ series. Each upload reflected his dedication and growth, from Day 1’s baby steps to Day 53’s advanced concepts. The repository didn’t just mark progress; it told a story of perseverance.

Notable GitHub Projects

Adil’s GitHub is a testament to his creativity and technical skills. Here are some of his standout projects:

  • Machine Learning Housing Corp: A project focused on predicting housing prices using advanced machine learning algorithms.
  • 100 Simple Websites: A collection of easy-to-build yet effective website designs showcasing web development fundamentals.
  • 50 Days of Machine Learning: A comprehensive series documenting Adil’s journey from beginner to advanced concepts in ML.
  • Harvard Website: A beautifully designed website concept dedicated to Harvard University.
  • Book Recommender System: An intelligent system that suggests books based on user preferences and reading history.
  • Cat vs Dog Image Classification Project: A deep learning model that classifies images of cats and dogs with high accuracy.

Triumphs and Challenges

Adil’s path was not without hurdles. Learning Machine Learning, Generative AI, and DSA from scratch tested his resilience. But with a dedicated coding tutor and a roadmap tailored to his needs, he steadily progressed from beginner to advanced levels. His efforts bore fruit when he cracked over 159 LeetCode problems before the end of 2024, sharing his triumphs on Twitter with his growing audience.

The Visionary

Adil’s vision extended beyond personal achievements. He aimed to use AI to help companies grow, imagining a future where his innovations could transform businesses. His entrepreneurial streak and technical prowess made him a force to reckon with in the tech world.

The Future Beckons

As 2025 began, each project reflected his growth, creativity, and relentless pursuit of excellence. Adil’s journey was far from over; it was just beginning. With Harvard on the horizon and his aspirations as his compass, Adil was poised to leave an indelible mark on the world.

Through my blog, I share lessons and stories from my journey, aiming to help others achieve innovation and success in AI and technology.
